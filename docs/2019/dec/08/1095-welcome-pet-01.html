What’s covered?

There are lots of benefits included in your Pet Insurance Policy, for full details please always read your Policy Schedule, however below are just some of the standard benefits that are available to you;

  • Veterinary fees which are covered each year including hospitalisation and referral up to €4000*
  • Discounts if your pet is microchipped or you have 3+ pets insured on your policy
  • €250,000 Third Party Liability cover if your pet caused a accident or injures somebody

*Standard excess of €100 applies once per condition per year, other voluntary excesses are available. Please note if your dog is over 7 or your cat is over 10 years old you will pay a percentage of the cost on each claim.

Is it hard to make a claim?

Not at all! Supporting you through making a claim is our main priority. No matter how big or small, just talk with our veterinary qualified claims team and they will guide you through the process from start to finish.

To notify us of a claim simply call 01 613 3990 or you can notify us through your MyAllianz account.

CLAIMS INFO
How much is standard excess?

Our standard excess is €100, but optional lower or higher excesses are available. Our customers can choose different excesses, so to find yours make sure to check your Policy Schedule. Remember the age of your dog matters; you may also pay a percentage amount of the vet fees in the unfortunate event that your pet has to see the vet.
